Web Design

As a web designer, I create a website's appearance, style, and frequently even content. For instance, appearance refers to the colors, text, and images utilized. Information's organization and categorization are referred to as its layout. An effective web design is user-friendly, aesthetically pleasing, and appropriate for the target audience and brand of the website. Many websites focus on keeping things simple so that viewers won't be distracted or confused by additional information and functionality. Removing as many potential sources of user annoyance as possible is a crucial factor to take into account because the foundation of a web designer's output is a site that gains and nurtures the trust of the target audience.

Web Developer

I am in charge of writing the code that "tells" a website how to operate in my capacity as a web developer. My websites are "user friendly," which simply means that they are simple for users to utilize. I also create website features, such as forms to collect emails or to send newsletters, paywalls to collect payment information, and messages to thank clients for their participation. I am knowledgeable in technology and am aware of how web servers and computers work. In addition, I have extensive knowledge of a variety of software packages, online applications, and computer languages.

Animator

As an animator, I create various images, or "frames," which, when placed in a specific order, provide the illusion of movement. Models, puppets, or computer or hand-drawn images can all be used to create the effects. I do animations and other special effects for everything from video games to advertisements.

Graphic Designer

As a graphic designer, I produce hand-drawn or computer-generated visual concepts to convey ideas that motivate, educate, and engage people. In order to create applications like commercials, brochures, magazines, and reports, I create the general layout and production design. Designers produce compositions to convey ideas and implement changes, whereas illustrators and graphic designers produce unique works of art.
